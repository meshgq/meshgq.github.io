The mental effects of ALCOHOLISM


  Alcoholism is a chronic disease caused by addiction to alcohol. Notice the use of the word CHRONIC. Chronic as used in this context is talking about a prolonged period of time, that is a problem that reoccures for a prolonged period of time. So therefore alcoholism is a prolonged or reoccuring disease caused by addiction to alcohol. Alcoholism is a disease, it eats you up like cancer. So the more you get involved in alcohols the more your alcoholic state worsen.
  Whenever alcoholism is mentioned anywere, what comes to our mind is the physical effects, such as dizziness, vomiting, talking out of terms, violence e.t.c. that is, the effects that are visible to the eye. The effects of alcoholism goes beyond the visible ones, there are severe effects to alcohlism such as the mental effects(ME) and this is what we will be discussing.
