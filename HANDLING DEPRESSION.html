HANDLING DEPRESSION
#HD


   Depression is a common but serious mood disorder. It's a state of mind producing serious long term lowering of enjoyment of life, happiness etc. It is the mother of sadness, anger, rage. It lowers your ability to visualize a happy future. It affects how you feel, think , and handle daily activities, such as sleeping, drinking, eating or working. 
  Depression is one of the most common mental disorder in Nigeria. As of November 2015 daily trusth recorded about 48 million Nigerians suffering from depression. Depression can happen at any age, but often begins in adult hood. But research now shows that depression now occurs in children and adolescents

~~Signs of depression~~

Depression if not handled properly can lead to suicide, low self esteem, low performance in school, fear of the of the unknown (future). How do i know if am depressed? Because depression is not written on the face. It is like a disease that creeps into your mental faculty slowly. It is like a brain tumor, a virus that affects your mind.
Some of the signs of depression include

1. Persistent sad, anxious or empty mood

2. Feelings of guilt, worthlessness, helplessness

3. Feelings of hopelessness

4. Appetite or weight loss

5. Moving or talking more slowly.
These and more are some of the signs of depression.
