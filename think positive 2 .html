THINK POSITIVE II

INSTRUCTIONS AND ADVICE

- Use your imagination to visualise only favourable and beneficial situations

- Use positive words in your inner dialogues, or when talking with others..very important

- Smile a little more, as this helps to think positively

- If a negative thought comes into your mind, quickly replace with a positive one.

- If a negative thought resists, do not give up, keep looking at the beneficial good and happy thoughts in your mind.

- Only expect positive results, circumstances and situations will change accordingly. 

- Your thinking affects your approach, remember to take away al the doubts. 

FOCUSING ON POSITIVE THINKING

* Identify areas to change

* Check yourself

* Be open to humor

* Follow a healthy lifestyle

* Surround yourself with positive people

* Practice positive self-talk

* Put positive thinking into practice everyday.
