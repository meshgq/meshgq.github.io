CAUSES AND EFFECTS OF DRUG ABUSE

  •       Drug abuse is the habitual taking of addictive or illegal drugs. It is also called substance abuse. Drug abuse is the compulsive, excessive, and self-damaging use of habit forming drugs or substances, leading to addiction or dependence. It can also cause serious damage to kidneys, liver or heart, also dysfunctional behavior patterns, hallucination, memory loss and death.

                    CAUSES OF DRUG ABUSE

* Use of drugs by friends/peers

* Availability of drugs from family and friends/peers

* Behavioral problems combined with poor parenting

* Emotional instability

* Social and academic pressure

* Inability to naturally cope with stress

* Low self-esteem or lack of confidence

* Frustration

* Misinformation

                             EFFECTS AND HEALTH CONSEQUENCES

* Heart or lung disease

* Cancer

* Addiction (brain disorder)

* Death

    Drug abuse can change how certain brain circuits work. These brain changes interfere with how people experience normal pleasures in life, their ability to control their stress level, decision making, and ability to learn and remember etc.

Drug abuse can also affect babies born to women who use drug while pregnant. Drug abuse can also affect one's education level, employment, relationships and criminal involvement. 

         WHY DRUG ABUSE IS BAD FOR YOU

We have seen from the above the causes and effects of drug abuse or substance abuse. Drug abuse affects your Mental, Emotional and Social Health (MESH) negatively. The continues abuse of drugs causes mental disorders and also organ failure in the body. Drug abuse can lead to suicide. It causes mental disorders; it can wreck you emotionally, destroy your social life and Put your health at risk.
